Sports Cards & Memorability

I have been a sports card collector since 1985 with over 140,000 cards dating back to 1951 without missing a year. Of course, the oldest one dating to 1933. Although I never kept much memorability when I was young except for a few autographs, I have kept a lot over the last thirty years. Even though it would be impossible to list everything I have there is a few that I can be shown. Although it is not a very impressive collection. These images are just a very small example, not even 1% of 1%, of a collection. Over 35 years of my life. These card collection spans over 70 years and the memorabilia goes back about 35 years.

Movies & Video Games

What can be said about movies and video games. Over the years I have bought alot, but in the end kept maybe half of the movies that I owned between VHS, DVD, and Blue-Ray. But my video game collection is something different. I have six old consoles: Atari 2600,. NES,. Super NES,. N64,. Gamecube,. and Playstation 2.. Except for the Gamecube, every gaming console that I have is original. As in out of the box where the Gamecube is secondhand. And I have held on to every console and almost every computer game over the years. I have always liked video games, but never concidered myself as a gamer. I am more of a collector. There are many great consoles out there but the the ones that have always been my favorite. The same goes with certain computer games. Although they are designed more for the Windows 95 and XP operating systems. I still play them from time to time. All I do is bring out the old system and play away.
